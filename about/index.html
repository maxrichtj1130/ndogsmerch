ABOUT NDOGS

NDOGS merch is a collection of egg-themed t-shirts and hoodies. Established in 2022, based in Malang, Indonesia. The creator chose eggs as a theme due to his love of eggs. The egg is a very simple dish used in culinary worlds all over the world. So that we present to you our egg-themed fashion collection..

All of our products have our signature designs on top of it. Each product has different unique designs and came in five different colors: white, black, sky-blue, chocolate, and yellow-green. The designs represent each of our collections: ceplogs edition and godogs edition. We will soon be enriching our products and collections

Our products are made for environmental and human-friendly reasons, our products are only made when you order them. Typically, our products will be made immediately, we will send you the tracking information as soon as they are shipped. We ship all over Indonesia.
